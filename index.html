Tourism

Roman Colossuem

The Roman Colosseum is definitely one of the best spots to visit in Rome. This location provides entertainment for citizens and tourists. It is also exciting to see the Colosseum in its prime.

Roman Bathhouses

Roman bathhouses are amazing and multipurpose. The bathhouses can be used for exercise, socializing, and bathing. If you get there really early, then you might just get the whole bath to yourself.

People

Virgil

Virgil was a very famous poet during the Pax Romana. Virgil is most commonly known for being the author of latin epic Aeneid.

Augustus (Octivian)

Augustus is the current emporer and the person responsible for the Pax Romana. He is also the son of Julius Caesar and he is the first Emporer of the roman empire.

Inventions

Aqueducts

The Romans are very famous for creating the very first aqueducts. The aqeducts were actually very advanced because they would decrease in only minor increments often times less than 4 meters for every kilometer.

Cement

Cement is a famous roman invention that is still used today for roads, statues, and even building houses. It was made from a mix of white powder, water, and rock fragments from volcanic eruptions. Cement was one of the most important roman discoveies.
